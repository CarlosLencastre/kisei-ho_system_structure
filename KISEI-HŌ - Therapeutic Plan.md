# 1. Purpose of the Therapeutic Plan

The Therapeutic Plan exists to:
- reflect the body’s current organisation
- describe structural and energetic tendencies
- outline a possible therapeutic direction
- support gradual reorganisation
- reinforce autonomy

It is descriptive, not prescriptive.

---
## 2. Structure of the Therapeutic Plan

The Therapeutic Plan must include:
- recap of the body’s organisation
- structural tendencies
- energetic tendencies
- axis behaviour
- integrated pattern

---
# 3. Language Rules

The Therapeutic Plan must:
- use calm, grounded, and sensory language
- avoid clinical, biomechanical, or prescriptive terms
- remain body-centred and descriptive
- use conditional language (e.g., "It may be helpful to...")
- avoid emotional interpretation
- avoid spiritual or esoteric language
- avoid prescriptive or corrective language
