## 4. Edge Case Validation

### Handling Conflicting Data
If structural and energetic data conflict (e.g., structural data suggests upper-dominant organization but energetic data suggests lower-dominant):
1. Prioritize Energetic Data: Energetic states often reflect the body's current adaptive response.
2. Cross-Reference with Axis: Check Centre, Root, and Firmament for grounding clues.
3. Consult Energetic Framework: Use global patterns to resolve ambiguity.
4. Document the Conflict: Note the discrepancy in the Integrated Analysis for therapist review.

### Examples of Edge Cases
 | Scenario | Resolution |
 |----------|------------|
 | Structural: Upper-dominant, Energetic: Lower-dominant | Prioritize energetic data; note structural tendency in narrative. |
 | Blockage in Spine + Excess in Shoulders | Describe as "compensatory effort in upper body due to central tension." |
 | Missing Data for a Region | Omit the region from analysis; do not speculate. |

### Validation Checklist
- All classifications align with Structural & Energetic Tables.
- Conflicts are resolved using the above rules.
- Edge cases are documented in the Integrated Analysis.
