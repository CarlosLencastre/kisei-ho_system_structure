## 3. Template Examples
Sample outputs for each template type to clarify usage.

### Example 1: Structural Reading Template
**Input**:
- Structural: Shoulders elevated (+12°), Spine neutral.
- Energetic: Shoulders show ⨂ (Blockage), Spine shows → (Normal Flow).

**Output**:
> Your body currently distributes effort toward your upper region, with a tendency to hold tension in the shoulders. This suggests a protective adaptation, where your upper body organizes to support stability and action. Your spine maintains a neutral flow, allowing for balanced movement.

### Example 2: Energetic Reading Template

## 4. Structural Rules

All templates must:
- Use short paragraphs (max 3–4 sentences).
- Avoid lists and numbers.
- Maintain a fluid rhythm.
- Use conditional language (e.g., "It may be helpful...").
