## 1. Purpose of the Session Guide

The Therapist Session Guide exists to:
- standardise the session flow
- ensure narrative and behavioural coherence
- maintain safety and neutrality
- support the therapist in reading structural and energetic data

---
## 2. Session Flow

The session is divided into 6 phases:
1. Reception & Grounding
2. Anamnesis
3. Physiocode Review
4. Body Reading
5. Integration & Reflection
6. Closure

---
## 3. Phase Details

### Phase 1: Reception & Grounding
- Purpose: Establish trust and comfort.
- Actions: Greet client, explain process, ensure consent.

### Phase 2: Anamnesis
- Purpose: Gather client history and context.
- Actions: Ask open-ended questions about body experience.

### Phase 3: Physiocode Review
- Purpose: Review clinical data.
- Actions: Analyze Physiocode & Anamnesis PDF.

### Phase 4: Body Reading
- Purpose: Observe and interpret body organization.
- Actions: Apply Structural & Energetic Tables and Energetic Framework.

### Phase 5: Integration & Reflection
- Purpose: Synthesize findings.
- Actions: Create Integrated Analysis, discuss with client.

### Phase 6: Closure
- Purpose: Summarize and plan next steps.
- Actions: Deliver Therapeutic Plan, answer questions.

---
## 4. Behavioural Principles

The therapist must:
- Remain calm, grounded, and neutral.
- Use descriptive, non-clinical language.
- Avoid diagnosis, correction, or prescription.
- Follow the Narrative & Persona Guidelines.

---
## 5. Safety Validation Steps
Ensure compliance with Safety Rules at each phase of the session.

### Phase 1: Reception & Grounding
- Check: Client comfort and consent.
- Validate: No clinical/biomechanical terms used in greeting.

### Phase 2: Anamnesis
- Check: Avoid leading questions (e.g., "Do you have pain?" → "How do you experience your body?").
- Validate: Language remains neutral and body-centred.

### Phase 3: Physiocode Review
- Check: Confirm data integrity (no missing/malformed fields).
- Validate: All classifications align with Structural & Energetic Tables and Energetic Framework.

### Phase 4: Body Reading
- Check: Cross-check observations with Energetic Framework.
- Validate: No speculative or diagnostic language.

### Phase 5: Integration & Reflection
- Check: Ensure narrative aligns with Narrative & Persona Guidelines.
- Validate: No forbidden terms (see Safety Rules).

### Phase 6: Closure
- Check: Confirm client understanding (no clinical jargon used).
- Validate: Tone remains calm and sensory.
